To enhance the **readability** and **visual clarity** of the `div` background and sentiment **badges** (positive, neutral, etc.) in light mode, here's a color suggestion palette that balances MoodMeter’s brand vibe with better accessibility and contrast:

---

### 🎨 **Card (div) Background Color Ideas**

| Purpose                    | Suggested Color   | Hex Code  | Notes                                      |
| -------------------------- | ----------------- | --------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| 🧾 Default card bg         | Soft Ice Blue     | `#EAF1FB` | Gentle tone, doesn't overpower the text    |
| 🔍 Hover state             | Light Indigo Tint | `#DCE6FF` | Slight highlight to indicate interactivity |
| ✍️ Text Color              | Charcoal          | `#1A1A1A` | Deep gray for better readability           |
| 💡 Divider line (optional) | Light Gray        | `#D1D5DB` | To separate articles cleanly               |

---

### 🏷️ **Badge Color Suggestions**

| Badge Type                          | Background            | Text Color             | Hex Codes Example         |
| ----------------------------------- | --------------------- | ---------------------- | ------------------------- |
| ✅ Positive                          | Mint Green `#D1FAE5`  | Dark Green `#065F46`   | Fresh & readable          |
| ⚠️ Neutral                          | Soft Gold `#FEF3C7`   | Golden Brown `#92400E` | Stands out gently         |
| ❌ Bearish                           | Blush Red `#FEE2E2`   | Burgundy `#991B1B`     | Warning tone              |
| 🧠 AI Analysis                      | Indigo Tint `#E0E7FF` | Indigo `#3730A3`       | Consistent with MoodMeter |
| 📊 Category tags (Tech, Economy...) | Slate Gray `#E5E7EB`  | Charcoal `#111827`     | Subtle and neutral        |

---

### 🧱 Tailwind CSS Utility (Example)

```html
<div class="bg-[#EAF1FB] hover:bg-[#DCE6FF] text-[#1A1A1A] border border-[#D1D5DB] rounded-xl p-4 shadow-sm transition-all">
  <!-- Card Content -->
</div>

<span class="bg-[#D1FAE5] text-[#065F46] text-sm font-medium px-3 py-1 rounded-full">positive</span>
```
